Explore the research landscape of WMiI UAM

Two interactive views over the same dataset: a PCA map of author embeddings grouped by research profile, and a publication-level visualiser that ranks dimensionality-reduction methods and lets you drill into individual papers. Click any author on the map to jump straight to their publications.

A
Authors Map

PCA projection of fine-tuned author embeddings. Each point is a research-profile cluster; many authors split into several. Click a point to inspect that author’s publications.

P
Publications Explorer

3 440 papers across 108 authors, projected by seven dimensionality-reduction methods. Browse the scatter, compare DR metrics, and inspect the optimal-k analysis.

Open publications explorer
How to use
  1. Start on the Authors Map to see how research profiles cluster.
  2. Click any author point → you’ll land on the Publications Explorer with that author already highlighted and their papers listed.
  3. On the Publications Explorer, switch between projections (UMAP, t-SNE, PaCMAP, …) or open the DR Metrics and K-Analysis tabs.
  4. Toggle dark / light mode from the icon in the top-right corner.
